Prophet Muhammad

the seal of the messengers and prophets of Allah.

Timeline of Muhammad's ﷺ life:

  • c. 570 - Death of his father, Abdullah.
  • c. 570 - Possible date of birth: 12 or 17 Rabi al Awal: in Mecca, Arabia.
  • c. 577 - Death of his mother, Amina.
  • c. 583 - His grandfather transfers him to Syria
  • c. 595 - Meets and marries Khadijah.
  • c. 599 - Birth of Zainab, his first daughter, followed by: Ruqayyah, Umm Kulthum, and Fatima Zahra.
  • 610 - Qur'anic revelation begins in the Cave of Hira on the Jabal an-Nour, the "Mountain of Light" near Mecca. At age 40, Angel Jebreel (Gabriel) was said to appear to Muhammad on the mountain and call him "the Prophet of Allah" | Begins in secret to gather followers in Mecca.
  • c. 613 - Begins spreading message of Islam publicly to all Meccans.
  • c. 614 - Heavy persecution of Muslims begins.
  • c. 615 - Emigration of a group of Muslims to Ethiopia.
  • c. 616 - Banu Hashim clan boycott begins.
  • 619 - Banu Hashim clan boycott ends. | The year of sorrows: Khadija (his wife) and Abu Talib (his uncle) die.
  • c.620 - Isra and Mi'raj (reported ascension to heaven to meet Allah)
  • 622 - Hijra, emigration to Medina (called Yathrib).
  • 624 - Battle of Badr.
  • 625 - Battle of Uhud.
  • 627 - Battle of the Trench (also known as the siege of Medina).
  • 628 - The Meccan tribe of Quraysh and the Muslim community in Medina sign a 10-year truce called the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah.
  • 630 - Conquest of Mecca.
  • 632 - Farewell pilgrimage, event of Ghadir Khumm, and death, in what is now Saudi Arabia
